# 🚀 Talla — Investor Pitch

## 1. Problem

People in Saudi Arabia constantly ask:

👉 “Where should we go right now?”

Current solutions fail:
- Google Maps → outdated reviews
- Instagram → not location-based
- TikTok → trends but not real-time nearby

❌ No platform shows **live social movement**

---

## 2. Solution

Talla is a real-time social discovery app that shows:

- where people are going right now
- what cafes are trending live
- which places are hotspots nearby

✅ Powered by:
- check-ins
- social activity
- trend scoring

---

## 3. Product

Talla combines:

- 📍 Location (where you are)
- 🔥 Trends (what’s hot)
- 👥 Social (who is there)

Into one experience.

---

## 4. How It Works

- Users check in at cafes
- Cafes get a trend score (/10)
- Areas become hotspots
- Users see live activity nearby

👉 More check-ins = higher trend

---

## 5. Market Opportunity

Saudi Arabia has:

- strong cafe culture
- trend-driven behavior
- high social media usage
- youth population

📈 Growing lifestyle economy under Vision 2030

---

## 6. Business Model

Talla makes money from cafes:

### 1. Paid visibility
- boost cafe ranking
- appear in hotspots

### 2. Data dashboard
- customer insights
- traffic analytics

### 3. Sponsored campaigns
- launches
- promotions
- events

---

## 7. Why Now?

- Saudi social behavior is trend-driven
- people follow “where the vibe is”
- cafes compete heavily for attention
- no real-time discovery platform exists

---

## 8. Competitive Advantage

Talla is:

- real-time (not static reviews)
- location-first (not random content)
- social-driven (not algorithm-only)

👉 It controls **physical traffic**

---

## 9. Vision

To become:

👉 The default platform in Saudi Arabia for discovering where people are going in real time.

Future:

- AI-powered recommendations
