# Talla — طلّة

## 🧠 What is Talla?

Talla is a Saudi-first social discovery platform that helps users find the trendiest cafes around them in real time.

Unlike traditional apps that rely on static ratings, Talla focuses on **live activity**, **check-ins**, and **social interaction** to determine what is actually trending.

---

## 🎯 Problem

People in Saudi Arabia often struggle with one question:

**“Where should we go right now?”**

Current solutions:
- Google Maps → outdated reviews
- Instagram → random and not location-based
- TikTok → trends but not real-time or nearby

There is no platform that answers:

👉 *“Where is the vibe right now near me?”*

---

## 💡 Solution

Talla solves this by:

- Tracking **real-time check-ins**
- Ranking cafes based on **live activity**
- Showing **hotspots instead of just locations**
- Integrating **social interaction into discovery**

---

## 🔥 Core Concept

Talla is built around 3 main pillars:

### 1. Live Trends
Cafes are ranked dynamically using:
- check-ins
- repeat visits
- social engagement
- friend activity

Each cafe gets a **trend score out of 10**

---

### 2. Location Intelligence
- Detects user location
- Shows cafes in the same area (especially dense cafe zones)
- Highlights **hotspot clusters** (not just individual cafes)

---

### 3. Social Layer
- Users can:
  - add friends
  - chat
  - post authentic moments
  - see where others are going

This transforms discovery into a **social experience**

---

## 🏆 Gamification

Users are rewarded through:

- Check-in points
- Streak bonuses (e.g. visiting same cafe 3 times)
- Badges (e.g. Hotspot Hunter)

This increases engagement and retention.

---

## 🌍 Vision

To become the **default platform in Saudi Arabia** for discovering where people are going in real time.

Long-term vision:
- Expand across GCC
- Become a lifestyle discovery platform (cafes → restaurants → events)
- Integrate AI-based recommendations
